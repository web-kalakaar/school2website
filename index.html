about us

Sant Kirpal Senior Secondary School is a trusted name in quality education in Bharatpur, dedicated to shaping bright futures with strong values and modern learning. Affiliated with RBSE, the school offers both English Medium and Hindi Medium education, ensuring every student learns in the environment they are most comfortable in.
We focus not only on academic excellence but also on the overall development of students through discipline, creativity, and practical knowledge. Our experienced teachers, supportive environment, and well-structured curriculum help students build confidence and achieve their goals.
At Sant Kirpal School, we believe that every child has potential — and with the right guidance, they can achieve great success. We are committed to creating responsible, knowledgeable, and confident individuals ready to face the future.
