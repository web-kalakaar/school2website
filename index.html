about us

Sant Kirpal Senior Secondary school, established in the year 2000, is committed to providing quality education and shaping the future of young minds. With a strong focus on academic excellence, discipline, and overall development, the school creates a supportive and inspiring learning environment. Under the visionary leadership of our Director, the institution continues to nurture talent through modern teaching methods, experienced faculty, and a values-based approach to education. Our goal is to empower students with knowledge, confidence, and skills to succeed in life.
